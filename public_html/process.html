Understanding the user

First I need to do a research. Would anyone need this app? After that I empathise with the potential users in oder to understand tham and to solve their problem with the project of my team. I find out whether he/she needs that kind of the app anyway? Has he/she had problems using similar apps on market? Is there a similar app?

Starting the design

After I figure out what are the main pain points of my personas and how they think, I start designing. First I make paper wireframes by sketching my Ideas, and then I start digitalising them with Figma in order to share it with my team and to discuss it.

User testing

When I have my wireframe prototypes done, I start testing them with users. Usually I send links from prototypes to 5-10 people to test it and to record me on camera their thoughts. Everytime I have 5 tasks they need to accomplish. It helps me a lot because I can hear and see how they interact with my app and see what are the potencial problems on witch I should still work on. This kind of testing is for me great because the participants can use the app in their own environment and without that feeling that they are watched.
